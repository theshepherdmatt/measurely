  • Quick, honest room measurement (offline Pi, no jargon)

How it Works

  1. Rent the kit (£50 + refundable £175 deposit)
  2. Plug in USB mic → go to measurely.local → press “Measure”
  3. Read Dave’s 3 plain-English nudges
  4. Move stuff, re-measure, hear the difference
  5. Re-pack, stick on the prepaid label, drop at Post Office
  6. Deposit refunded within 24 hours of safe return
