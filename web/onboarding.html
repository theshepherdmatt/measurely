First-Time Setup

Let's get your room dialled in.

Before we run your first sweep, Dave needs a few details about your room and speaker layout. Don’t worry — he’ll guide you through it step by step. Grab a tape measure (or use the Measure app on your iPhone) and we’ll get started.

1

Measure Your Room

Jot down the length, width, and height of your room. Close enough is fine — no need for millimetre precision.

2

Measure Listening Position

Measure the distance between your ears and each speaker. Distance between the speakers helps too.

3

Fill Out Room Setup

Enter the measurements into the Room Setup page so Dave understands your space before he analyses it.

Go to Room Setup
4

Run Your First Sweep

Once Room Setup is complete, head to the dashboard and run your first sweep. Dave will score your room and explain everything in simple English.
