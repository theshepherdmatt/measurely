Frequency Band Guide

Bass (20-200Hz): Low frequencies, bass instruments, subwoofer range

Mid (200-2kHz): Most instruments, vocals, critical for clarity

Treble (2-10kHz): High frequencies, cymbals, consonants, brightness

Air (10-20kHz): Ultra-high frequencies, spatial cues, sparkle
